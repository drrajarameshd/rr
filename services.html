Internal Medicine — Services

Diabetes

What we do: Diagnosis, glycaemic optimisation, insulin initiation & titration, continuous glucose monitoring guidance, diabetic foot exam, counselling for diet & exercise, pregnancy diabetes care.

Common tests: Fasting & postprandial glucose, HbA1c, lipid profile, urine microalbumin, renal profile, ECG (if indicated).

Urgent: signs of DKA — immediate ER/urgent admission.

Book Diabetes Consultation
Thyroid Disorders

What we do: Interpret TSH/T4/T3, manage hypo/hyperthyroidism, dose adjustments, monitoring in pregnancy, and investigation of nodules with specialist referral.

Common tests: TSH, Free T4, Free T3, anti-TPO/anti-Tg, thyroid ultrasound referral when needed.

Upload Thyroid Report
Hypertension

What we do: BP optimisation, home/ambulatory BP guidance, medication selection and side-effect management, secondary hypertension workup and lifestyle modification plans.

Common tests: Ambulatory BP monitoring, renal function, electrolytes, urinalysis, ECG.

Urgent: hypertensive emergency with organ dysfunction — seek immediate ER/ICU care.

Asthma & COPD

What we do: Diagnose and classify severity, inhaler technique training, personalised action plans, nebuliser vs inhaler guidance, long-term controller optimisation.

Common tests: Spirometry, peak flow, chest X-ray, pulse oximetry.

Urgent: severe breathlessness or cyanosis — go to ER/ICU.

OP · IP · ICU Care

What we do: Outpatient evaluation, inpatient rounds, ICU consults (ventilator advice, sepsis management), multidisciplinary coordination and family updates.

Call for Urgent Consult
Cardiovascular Risk & Lipids

What we do: ASCVD risk stratification, lipid management, lifestyle interventions, statin counselling and secondary prevention coordination.

Chronic Kidney Disease & Electrolytes

What we do: CKD detection, slowing progression, dose adjustments, electrolyte management, dialysis liaison.

Urgent: severe hyperkalaemia or fluid overload — urgent hospital care.

Infectious Diseases — OP & IP

What we do: Diagnosis and antibiotic stewardship, managing pneumonia, UTI, skin infections, and coordinating IV antibiotics / blood cultures when inpatient.

Common tests: CBC, cultures, CRP/ESR, chest X-ray.

Anemia & Hematologic Evaluation

What we do: Workup for iron/B12/folate deficiency, chronic disease anemia, transfusion decisions, and hematology referral when needed.

Common tests: CBC, peripheral smear, iron studies, B12/folate.

Autoimmune & Inflammatory Conditions

What we do: Initial screening and coordination for suspected autoimmune disease, interpretation of ANA/ENA and inflammatory markers, co-management with rheumatology.

Geriatric Care & Multimorbidity

What we do: Holistic assessment, polypharmacy review, falls assessment, cognitive screening, advance care planning and home-care coordination.

Obesity & Metabolic Syndrome

What we do: Metabolic assessment, individualized weight-management plans (diet, activity, pharmacotherapy), and bariatric referral when indicated.

Stroke Prevention & Neurologic Risk

What we do: AF detection & anticoagulation counselling, BP & lipid control for stroke prevention and coordination with neurology.

Urgent: sudden weakness or slurred speech — call emergency services immediately.

ECG & Arrhythmia Evaluation

What we do: 12-lead ECG interpretation, Holter/event monitor referrals, initial arrhythmia management and cardiology coordination.

Preventive Medicine & Vaccinations

What we do: Adult immunisations (influenza, pneumococcal, hepatitis, Tdap), health screening packages and lifestyle counselling.

Travel Medicine & Pre-travel Advice

What we do: Risk assessment, vaccine recommendations, malaria prophylaxis guidance and chronic disease planning while abroad.

Interpretation of Lab Reports & Treatment Plans

What we do: Detailed report review, plain-language explanation, recommended investigations and stepwise treatment plans for chronic and acute conditions. Remote upload option available.

Upload Reports for Review
Medication Review & Polypharmacy

What we do: Reconcile medicines, reduce inappropriate polypharmacy, adjust doses for organ function and avoid harmful interactions—especially in older adults.

Care Coordination & Second Opinions

What we do: Coordinate multidisciplinary care, provide second opinions, and prepare clear discharge & follow-up plans after hospital stays.

Request Care Coordination
How to access our services

Walk-in / OP: Routine consultations by appointment. IP / ICU: we provide inpatient consults—call the hospital for urgent transfers. Remote review: secure report upload for written management recommendations.
